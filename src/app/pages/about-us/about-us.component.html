What is nodebucket?

nodebucket is a web-based, Kanban-style, list-making application that allows you to manage your tasks and stay organized. As the fastest growing task management application on the market, it provides project managers with real-time visibility on project status.

Add new tasks

Add new tasks you want to track with a simple click of the mouse. Tasks can be organized in one of three buckets--to-do, in progress, and done. The drag-and-drop feature makes it easy and fast to update task statuses.

Manage tasks with ease

Cards keep your tasks organized and in one place. As you start or complete a task, just drag it over to the correct new column. Team members can track their work tasks at every stage, and nodebucket gives them the power to revise and update tasks at any time, keeping true to the continuous improvement and continuous delivery values of Agile and DevOps

It's secure

With secure login, your tasks are only seen by you.
